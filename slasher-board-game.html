SLASHER Board Game

Board Game Game Design Horror Multiplayer

A thrilling multiplayer board game inspired by classic slasher films, where players must work together to survive against a relentless threat. Will you escape or become the next victim?

Project Overview

SLASHER is a cooperative survival horror board game designed for 3-6 players. Drawing inspiration from classic slasher films of the 70s and 80s, players take on the roles of stereotypical characters trying to survive the night against a deadly killer. Each player has unique abilities and weaknesses, encouraging teamwork and strategic planning to escape alive.

What sets SLASHER apart is its dynamic gameplay that creates tension through unpredictable events and the constant threat of the killer, who becomes more powerful as the game progresses. Will you risk helping your friends or save yourself?

Key Features

Character Roles

Each player takes on a unique character archetype with special abilities and disadvantages, creating varied gameplay experiences with different group compositions.

Escalating Terror

As the game progresses, the killer becomes more powerful and unpredictable, increasing tension and forcing players to adapt their strategies.

Dynamic Gameplay

No two games are alike with randomized map layouts, event cards, and killer behavior creating a highly replayable experience.

Strategic Choices

Players must balance gathering resources, completing objectives, and evading the killer while deciding when to help teammates or save themselves.

Multiple Escape Routes

Several ways to win create different strategic paths and encourage diverse approaches to survival in each game.

Horror Movie Atmosphere

Artwork, mechanics, and events are designed to evoke the feeling of being in a classic slasher film, complete with tropes and unexpected twists.

Development Process

January 2023

Concept Development

Initial game concept and mechanics were drafted, focusing on creating tension and meaningful player choices in a horror setting.

March 2023

Prototype Testing

First paper prototype created and tested with focus groups to refine core gameplay mechanics and balance character abilities.

May 2023

Art Direction

Established visual style and started artwork creation for game board, cards, and character pieces with a retro horror aesthetic.

August 2023

Expanded Playtesting

Wider playtesting with multiple groups to identify balance issues and refine gameplay dynamics between survivors and killer.

November 2023

Final Development

Finalizing rulebook, game components, and preparing for production with feedback from extensive playtesting incorporated.

Current Status

Pre-Production

The game is currently in late development with plans for crowdfunding to support production in the coming months.
